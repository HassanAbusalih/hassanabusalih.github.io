About Me

Hi! I'm Hassan, a game programmer at the end of my Bachelor’s degree at SAE Dubai, specialized in C# and Unity. I enjoy creating engaging gameplay mechanics through clean and efficient code, which is largely why I've always been the lead programmer in my collaborative projects. In the three years since I began learning game development, I’ve also developed skills in game/level design, shaders, and VFX.

I always try to achieve the highest quality possible in anything I work on, which pushes me to constantly learn new things and explore the limits of the tools I use. I'm passionate about games, and I love when a game goes the extra mile for those little details. As a result, I'm naturally driven to always do the same.
